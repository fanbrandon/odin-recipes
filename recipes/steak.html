Pan Seared, Butter Basted Steak

Ingredients

  • New York Strip
  • Olive Oil
  • Butter
  • Thyme
  • Rosemary
  • Garlic
  • Salt and Pepper

Instructions

  1. Season the steak
  2. Preheat the pan and then sear on side of the steak for 2 minutes
  3. Flip and repeat until cook to desired doneness
  4. Put butter, garlic, thyme and rosemary in pan then baste steak
  5. Leave steak to rest
  6. Serve
