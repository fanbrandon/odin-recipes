Spagetti and Meatballs

Ingredients

  • Tomato Sauce
  • Spagetti
  • Salt, Pepper, Garlic powder, onion powder, paprika
  • Panko Bread Crumbs
  • Egg
  • Parmesan Cheese

Instructions

  1. Make meatballs by combining seasonings, ground beef, panko and eggs
  2. Form into balls
  3. Cook pasta
  4. Sear and cook meatballs
  5. Heat up sauce and place meatballs in sauce to finishing cooking
  6. After pasta is cooked, toss in sauce
  7. Plate pasta with meatballs and grate cheese over
  8. Serve
