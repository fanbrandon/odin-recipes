Chicken Katsu and Rice

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Eggs
  • Panko Bread Crumbs
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Garlic Powder
  • Flour

Instructions

  1. Make a flour mixture with the salt, pepper and garlic powder
  2. Make an egg wash with beaten eggs
  3. Make a panko mix with seasonings
  4. Coat chicken in flour, then egg, then, panko
  5. Preheat oil to 350F
  6. Drop breaded chicken in oil until golden, repeat for all pieces
  7. Serve
